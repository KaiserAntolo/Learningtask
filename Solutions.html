The Philippine's police system poses a high risk of corruption, with the Philippines National Police (PNP) considered to be one of the most corrupt institutions within the country.

Punishment for corruption

Any public officer or private person committing any of the unlawful acts or omissions enumerated in Sections 3, 4, 5 and 6 of this Act shall be punished with imprisonment for not less than one year nor more than ten years, perpetual disqualification from public office, and confiscation or forfeiture of badge or any kind of identification that would relate them to being law enforcement.
